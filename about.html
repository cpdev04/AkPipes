Welcome To AK Pipes

AK Pipes and Sanitary House has been synonymous with quality products. We are one of the fastest-growing companies in India, offering diverse plumbing choices with our corporate office located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. As leaders in the distribution and supply of branded CPVC pipes and fittings, UPVC pipes and fittings, O-PVC pipes and fittings, HDPE pipes and fittings, PE-RT pipes and fittings, water storage tanks, borewell pipes and fittings, garden hose pipes, and sewerage pipes and fittings, we strive to maintain our reputation for excellence.

We have always been associated with the most reputed brands, such as Ori-Plast, Ashirvad, Supreme, Jaguar, Essco, Cera, Handware,Artize,Xen, Nirali, and WaterTec. Hence, our product line assures you the quality at the best price you have in mind. Quality and good service are AK Pipes and Sanitary House's success partners.

Functionality meets perfection

AK Pipes and Sanitary House maintains substantial inventories with wide product ranges and the highest service level standards, enabling our customers to enjoy the benefits. We see the world through the eyes of customers and deliver value as defined and measured by customers. We always look for long-term relationships with them.
